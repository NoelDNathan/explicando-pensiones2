## Checksums

Los checksums SHA-256 se recalculan sobre todos los ficheros de `data/raw/` y `data/processed/` y se guardan en `data/checksums.sha256`.

Comando usado en PowerShell:

```powershell
Get-ChildItem -Recurse -File data/raw,data/processed |
  Sort-Object FullName |
  Get-FileHash -Algorithm SHA256 |
  ForEach-Object { "$($_.Hash.ToLower())  $((Resolve-Path -Relative $_.Path).TrimStart('.\'))" }
```
